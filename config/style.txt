# STYLE CHARACTERISTICS OBSERVED:

## Tone & Voice:
- **Educational but accessible** - Not academic, conversational yet authoritative
- **Direct address** - Uses "אנחנו", "אתם" to engage readers
- **Confident without arrogance** - "אני לא מומחה" disclaimers but speaks with authority
- **Question-driven** - Opens with hooks: "מה זה...", "למה...", "איך..."

## Structure:
- **Clear progression**: Setup → Explanation → Takeaway/Lesson
- **Numbered lists** for multi-step processes
- **Double line breaks** for readability (very important!)
- **"נניח ש..."** (Let's assume...) for hypothetical examples
- **Short paragraphs** - 1-3 sentences max

## Language Mix:
- **Technical terms in English**: S&P500, IRR, Hedge, ETF, CEO
- **Hebrew translations**: מינוף, גידור, תשואה פנימי
- **Numbers & math**: Calculations, percentages, specific figures
- **Hebrew financial terms**: ש״ח, אג״ח, תיק השקעות

## Emoji Usage:
- **Minimal** - Professional, text-focused
- **No emojis in educational threads** (leverage, hedging, recessions)
- Occasionally in lighter content but very restrained

## Disclaimers:
- **Always include**: "*אין באמור לעיל המלצה לפעולה*"
- Position at START of sensitive content (SEC cases, specific stocks)

## Opening Hooks:
- **Conversational starter**: "אתמול אשתי שאלה אותי..."
- **Stats/Numbers**: "יותר מ4100 אנשים ענו..."
- **Current event**: "השוק מתקרב חזרה לשיא..."
- **Historical**: "ב1997 סטיב ג'ובס חזר לאפל..."

## Closing Patterns:
- **Clear takeaway**: "זאת העוצמה ש..."
- **Lesson/Application**: "מה אפשר ללמוד מזה"
- **Rhetorical question**: "אז מעכשיו ממנפים הכל, לא?"
- **Thread indicator**: "השרשור הכי חשוב שכתבתי"

## Critical Rules for Translation:
1. **NEVER translate**: S&P500, IRR, ETF, Hedge, CEO, IPO, VC, API
2. **Keep Hebrew financial terms**: ש״ח, אג״ח, מניות, תיק השקעות
3. **Preserve double line breaks** (critical for readability!)
4. **Use "נניח ש..." for examples** (Let's assume...)
5. **Direct address in plural**: אנחנו, אתם (we, you plural)
6. **Numbers in context**: "250 אלף", "21.4%", "$150M"
7. **No emojis** unless original has them
8. **Include disclaimers** when discussing specific stocks/legal cases
